2009 - 2010 Season

H. M. S. Pinafore
or Lass that Loved a Sailor
October 30 & 31; November 4, 6, & 7 at 8:00 pm
October 31 & November 7 at 2:00 pm
H. M. S. Pinafore is among the most popular of the Gilbert and Sullivan operas, perhaps because of its infectious tunes and generally well-constructed libretto. Drawing on several of his earlier "bab ballad" poems, Gilbert embued H. M. S. Pinafore with mirth and silliness to spare. The opera's gentle satire reprises and builds upon a sure to please comedic theme: Love between members of different social classes.
